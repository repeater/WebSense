What is Web security?

The fast-moving Web calls for fast-paced security.

Almost 2 billion people set a frantic pace on the Web every day. When your employees jump into the fray to search, post, and click, they carry your company data with them. Static security solutions just can't keep up with the pace, your employees, or your data. You need 24/7 protection in real time.

websense®
WEB SECURITY

You need security that keeps up.
And keeps you in compliance.

  • The best Web security solutions:
  • Protect your data 24/7 in all types of Web situations, including social media, Web apps, and links in email
  • Ensure compliance with company, industry, and government regulations
  • Increase productivity by giving employees the freedom to work on the Web with either limited or no access to personal sites

Websense Web security gives you all this – at the lowest overall cost.

Recommended

For full protection against today's (and tomorrow's) threats, choose a Web security gateway. These products offer the most comprehensive Web solution available, with real-time content monitoring so it's safe for your employees to click links in email, recruit new staff through LinkedIn, conduct research using Google, and more.


Core protection

Move up to true Web security. These solutions build on Web filtering with control for more than 125 network protocols and thousands of applications. But you won't have protection from today's ever-increasing blended threats that combine Web, email, and data attacks.



Just the basics

At the very least, you need Web filtering to prevent or limit access to unproductive or malicious sites. But you'll still be vulnerable to threats from social networking, Web mail, and Web apps.

What is email security?

Email can lead to all kinds of trouble.

Nearly 1.5 billion email users send 247 billion messages a day, almost 3 million messages a second. And 80% of those messages are spam, often containing links that lead to poison websites. The other 20% carry dangers, too, such as infected files and invasive scripts.

websense®
EMAIL SECURITY

Email invaders want your data.
Email security keeps it safe.

  • The best email security solutions:
  • Scrutinize your inbound and outbound emails 24/7, snatching anything that could pose a security threat
  • Include Web and data security too, to guard against threats that combine multiple avenues of attack
  • Let you set tight controls to ensure compliance with company, industry, and government regulations
  • Increase productivity by providing automatic backup, screening out unnecessary emails, and preventing costly and time-consuming security breaches

Websense email security gives you all this – at the lowest overall cost.

What is data security?

If you don't safeguard data, it'll cost you.

Security breaches occur all the time – most companies experience a breach at least once a week. And a single breach can easily cost $1 million or more in remediation. These days, you simply can't afford to be without data security.

websense®
DATA SECURITY

Guard your data with your life.
Better yet, get Websense®.

  • The best data security solutions:
  • Protect your data both in your databases and also on the go via email, Web posts, printers, laptops, and mobile devices
  • Give you embedded, enterprise-class protection against blended threats that combine multiple avenues of attack
  • Let you set tight controls to ensure compliance with company, industry, and government regulations
  • Increase productivity by preventing costly and time-consuming security breaches
  • Can be completely up and running in mere days, not weeks or months

Websense data security gives you all this – at the lowest overall cost.
