ACE is why no one stops more threats than Websense.

Websense® ACE delivers real-time security ratings to all products built on the Websense TRITON™ architecture. Its seven assessment areas and a unique composite scoring process enable TRITON solutions to detect things other security solutions miss.

ACE intelligence helps our information security products provide:

  • Protection against advanced threats, modern malware and data theft.
  • Productivity controls that understand social media and other applications.
  • Compliance through strong outbound content visibility and containment controls.

ACE is maintained by Websense Security Labs™ researchers. Its unique capabilities include:

  • Predictive security engines that can “see” developing trends.
  • Contextual assessments that help ensure accuracy.
  • Inline operation that tackles HTTPS and social media threats.
  • More than 10,000 analytics to enable deep inspections.
  • Composite scoring to support effective decision making.

ACE uses seven defense assessment areas. These provide checks and balances to minimize false positives and improve accurate classification. They are:

  • Real-Time Security Classification. ACE includes social media controls, and inspects web content for suspicious code.
  • Real-Time Content Classification. ACE employs advanced machine learning to quickly and accurately classify pages based on content.
  • Real-Time Data Classification. ACE classifies data with paring and decoding support to address outbound data theft.
  • Anti-Malware Engines. ACE applies multiple anti-malware engines to identify general and specialized malware.
  • Reputation Analysis. ACE uses more than twenty characteristics for assessment and reputation scoring that encompasses contextual awareness.
  • URL Classification. ACE uses this to apply current classification information for known pages, or to help assess new pages and links.
  • Anti-Spam/Spear-Phishing. ACE provides matchless protection against traditional and emerging email threats.
